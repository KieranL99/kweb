Why CorsoCoin?

The Key Tokenomic Factors  

Foundationally Yield Bearing

Perpetual non-intrusive staking that reflects 2% back to CORSO hodler's with future integration into CorsoKennel, the County's first staking protocol, the first of many!.

Transparency and Safety

Our protocols are internally audited and will receive external audits as we progress but we stand by the statement that we will become the protectorate of the people. Our actions will convey this message more so than these words.

Ponzi-nomics? Not with us!

With the burn address receiving its 2% and CorsoKennel receiving a portion of reflections these addresses lower supply, one permanently and one temporarily that release supply gradually.

Our Yellow brick road

We aspire to implement a yieldful, highly secure and well trusted eco-system(a County, in our case). These are the initial steps of that grand plan.

  • July 6th

    First Bark 

    Deployment of CorsoCoin with 30B tokens burned initially, before we renounce ownership of the CorsoCoin contract.

  • July 15th

    CorsoCoin Staking is released!     

    CorsoCounty's first (second) yield bearing faculty! Stake CORSO to receive CORSO tokens, If holders decide not to stake, this lowers circulating supply further which is only farmable by locking up CORSO which removes more from circulating supply. Ideally, holders will stake to increase yield.

  • July 17th

    Community Governance

    Experimental increased price floor taxed reflection governance token is released as a yield offering through Mollosus!

  • Aug 1st

    New Buddies!

    Community involvement taken to extremes! Voted-for members of the community will be invited to be part of the core team in developing, strategising or illustrating depending on the outcome of the vote!
